Why Re2?

Seamless Cross-Chain Deployment

Restaking participants deploy their own contracts on their preferred blockchain, seamlessly connecting to Re2. Utilizing a chain-agnostic message passing layer, Re2 ensures smooth cross-chain communication, enabling validators and delegators to interact effortlessly across multiple blockchain networks.

Comprehensive Risk Analysis Suite

Re2 equips users with a comprehensive suite of risk analysis tools, delivering key metrics such as leverage, creditworthiness, stake overlap, and more. This empowers users to make well-informed decisions when managing their positions, ensuring they have the insights needed to optimize their staking strategies and navigate restaking with confidence.

Decentralized Governance and Transparency

Re2 fosters a culture of decentralized governance and transparency, giving users a voice in the evolution of the platform. Through community-driven decision-making processes and transparent governance mechanisms, participants can actively shape the future of Re2.
